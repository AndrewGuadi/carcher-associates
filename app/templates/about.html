About the Firm

A consulting practice built on coordination, detail, and long-term usability.

The firm’s work centers on telecommunications and low voltage systems for institutional and commercial projects. The approach is grounded in clear documentation, practical construction coordination, and systems that support owner standards over time.

Philosophy

Detail matters because coordinated documents reduce uncertainty in bidding and construction. The design work stays focused on owner needs, maintainability, and realistic execution in the field.

Documentation

Drafting
