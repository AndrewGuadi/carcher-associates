Firm Overview

A consulting practice shaped by project coordination rather than marketing language.

C. Archer & Associates supports telecommunications and low voltage systems for institutional and commercial projects. The work includes coordination of bid documents, review of contractor submittals, and site involvement during construction.

The objective is straightforward: produce clear documents, support owner standards, and help reduce avoidable changes and delays.

Technical Tools

  • AutoCAD drafting support
  • Revit coordination where required
  • Construction-phase review
  • Owner-focused design standards
