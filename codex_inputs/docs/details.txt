Suggested palette direction:

Content Treatment Rules

Do not invent wild marketing copy.
Do not make the company sound like a startup agency.

The copy should feel like an engineering consultancy:

factual

capable

precise

calm

experienced

Good copy style:

“Our work includes healthcare, education, municipal, and institutional environments.”

“We provide consulting, documentation review, and drafting support.”

Avoid copy like:

“We transform your business”

“Next-generation innovation powerhouse”

“Cutting-edge disruption”

“Scale faster with seamless solutions”

Wrong tone.
